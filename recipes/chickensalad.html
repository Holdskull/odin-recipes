Chicken salad

Check out this delicious and practical recipe for simple chicken sausage! This dish is a true explosion of flavors that delights palates and is perfect for various occasions, from quick meals to special celebrations. With simple and easy-to-find ingredients, chicken salpicão combines the tender texture of shredded chicken with the crunch of straw potatoes and fresh vegetables, resulting in an irresistible mix. To prepare this dish, you will need cooked and shredded chicken breast, grated carrots, chopped apple, raisins, green corn, peas, mayonnaise and full-green to enhance the flavors. This recipe is an excellent option for those looking for practicality in the kitchen, as it is quick to make and does not require advanced culinary skills. Chicken salpicão is versatile and can be served as a side dish, filling sandwiches, a snack at parties or as a main dish. Its mix of textures and flavors will conquer your taste buds and that of everyone at the table. Follow the very simple step by step of this recipe and enjoy this simply irresistible dish!

Ingredients:
  • 500g of shredded chicken
  • 1 can of peas
  • 200g of raisins
  • mayonnaise
  • chips
  • 2 grated carrots
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 apple
  • coriander
It takes around 20 minutes to make
  1. Mix all the ingredients but the chips
  2. Cover it all wit a layer of chips
  3. It's ready to eat!!!
